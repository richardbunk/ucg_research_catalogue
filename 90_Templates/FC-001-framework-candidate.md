## Purpose

A Framework Candidate defines a proposed modification to the Unified Composition Guide based upon an approved Framework Integration Assessment.

Framework Candidates translate institutional knowledge into proposed framework implementation.

They do not establish canonical authority.

They serve as structured design proposals for editorial review.

## 6. Architectural Compatibility

Evaluate compatibility with the existing framework.

Consider consistency with governance, terminology, duplication, dependency, modularity, extensibility, and backward compatibility.

The proposal should strengthen rather than complicate the framework.

## Design Principle

Framework Candidates propose implementation.

They do not establish institutional knowledge.

They derive their authority from accumulated knowledge, structured organization, and approved Framework Integration Assessments.

The Unified Composition Guide evolves through deliberate architectural design rather than isolated editorial insight.
