## Purpose

A Framework Integration Assessment evaluates whether accumulated institutional knowledge justifies a change to the Unified Composition Guide.

The FIA serves as the editorial decision point between the Research Catalogue and the framework.

Its purpose is to ensure that framework evolution remains evidence-based, deliberate, and architecturally consistent.

An FIA does not modify the framework.

It determines whether a Framework Candidate should be created.

## Editorial Principle

Framework evolution should occur only after sufficient institutional knowledge has accumulated to justify architectural change.

The Framework Integration Assessment protects the stability of the Unified Composition Guide by ensuring that implementation follows understanding rather than isolated observations.
