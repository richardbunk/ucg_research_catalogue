3. Original Observation

4. Editorial Interpretation

Explain the significance of the observation.

Questions to consider:

- Why does this matter?
- What principle is represented?
- What recurring pattern does it suggest?
- How does it contribute to understanding?

Interpretation begins transforming information into institutional knowledge.
