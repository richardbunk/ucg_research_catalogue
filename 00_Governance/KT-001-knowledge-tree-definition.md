Purpose

A Knowledge Tree defines a persistent domain of institutional knowledge within the Unified Composition Guide Research Catalogue.

Knowledge Trees organize related Knowledge Articles into coherent bodies of understanding independent of framework implementation.

Their purpose is to preserve the long-term structure of institutional knowledge while allowing framework architecture to evolve independently.

Architectural Principle

Knowledge Trees organize institutional understanding.

They do not prescribe framework implementation.

Framework architecture may evolve independently while Knowledge Trees preserve the continuity, organization, and relationships of institutional knowledge.
