Purpose

The Editorial Review Record documents the formal evaluation of a Framework Candidate prior to canonical adoption within the Unified Composition Guide.

Editorial Review verifies that a proposed implementation:

- faithfully represents the supporting institutional knowledge,
- satisfies architectural expectations,
- complies with governance,
- and is suitable for publication as canonical framework.

Editorial Review is the final quality gate before framework authority is established.

Implementation Notes

---

Editorial Principle

Editorial Review validates implementation.

It does not recreate research, reinterpret institutional knowledge, or redesign framework architecture.

Its purpose is to ensure that approved implementation faithfully reflects accumulated institutional knowledge while preserving the integrity, coherence, and long-term maintainability of the Unified Composition Guide.
